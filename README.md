# decision-intelligence-latam
How data-driven decisions impact business outcomes in Latin America. A deep analysis of Mercado Libre, Nubank and Amazon LATAM.
aaa
## About this project

This is a personal portfolio project where I explore how data shapes 
business decisions in Latin America. My background is in risk and 
consulting, not engineering, and this project is my way of combining 
that business experience with data analysis.

I analyze three companies that represent different approaches to 
data-driven decision making in the region: Mercado Libre, Nubank, 
and Amazon LATAM.

## Data sources

All analysis is based on publicly available information including 
annual reports, regulatory filings, investor relations documents, 
earnings calls, and reputable financial and business publications.

## What I am exploring

- How each company uses data to make key business decisions
- Where data-driven approaches create measurable advantages
- Patterns and gaps in decision making across different business models

## Scope

**Period:** Fiscal Year 2024

**Markets analyzed:** Brazil and Mexico

**Companies:**
- Mercado Libre
- Nubank
- Amazon LATAM

**Data sources:** Official investor relations reports, SEC filings, 
central bank publications, and reputable financial media.

Python, SQL, data visualization
